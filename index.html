At an extraordinary historical moment, Wayne Theibaud proposed a radical new take on painting, and he did so with a slice of pie. This exhibition invites viewers to trace Theibaud's emergence as a mature artist with a singular style. The first exhibition to explore this formative period, Wayne Theibaud | 1958-1968 brings together more than 60 early paintings gathered from private collections and museums throughout the United States
